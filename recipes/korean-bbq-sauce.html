Korean Style BBQ Sauce

Description

The following is a recipe to be used along with the included Crunchy Tofu recipe to create what is known as the Ozmus dish, "The Crunchy Stuff".

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp. Minced garlic
  • 6 tbsp. Water
  • 6 tbsp. Soy sauce
  • 1.5 tbsp. Rice wine vinegar
  • 2 tbsp Mirin
  • 1.5 tbsp. Chili garlic sauce
  • 1.5 tsp. Cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Heat cooking oil in wok or large skillet
  2. other ingredients together.
  3. Use remaining frying oil and pour in sauce.
  4. Stir constantly and get sauce to a boil.
  5. Continue cooking until thick.
