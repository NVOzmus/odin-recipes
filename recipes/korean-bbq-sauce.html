Korean Style BBQ Sauce

3/4 cup sugar 2 tbsp. Minced garlic 6 tbsp. Water 6 tbsp. Soy sauce 1.5 tbsp. Rice wine vinegar 2 tbsp Mirin 1.5 tbsp. Chili garlic sauce 1.5 tsp. Cornstarch 2 tbsp Cooking oil Heat cooking oil in wok or large skillet Mix other ingredients together. Use remaining frying oil and pour in sauce. Stir constantly and get sauce to a boil. Continue cooking until thick.
