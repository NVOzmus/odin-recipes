Crunchy Tofu

  • 14 oz tofu
  • 3/4 cup constarch
  • 1/4 cup cornmeal
  • Paprika
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Garlic powder
  • Pepper
  • Oil to fry
  1. Drain tofu. Cut into cubes.
  2. Place on paper towel lined baking sheet and let drain for 20 minutes.
  3. Pat dry with with paper towel.
  4. Mix together dry ingredients.
  5. Heat up oil in large skillet, approximately 1 inch.
  6. Place tofu in breading and coat pieces thoroughly.
  7. Fry tofu until crispy, then turn over.
  8. Allow about 1-2 minutes each side.
